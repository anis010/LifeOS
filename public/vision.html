Our Vision

One life, unified through technology, guided by wisdom

"Technology should amplify human wisdom, not replace it. My System exists to make you the hero of your own life story."
— The Founders of My System

In a world of fragmented apps and scattered data, we believe in the power of unity. Your life is interconnected—your tools should be too. My System is not just another app; it's your digital companion in the journey of becoming your best self.

Core Vision

Unified Life Experience

Break down the silos between different life domains. Your physical health affects your mental clarity, which impacts your career performance, which influences your financial decisions. My System sees the connections and helps you optimize holistically.

Privacy-First Philosophy

Your life data is yours alone. We use local-first architecture with encrypted sync, ensuring you maintain complete control. No data mining, no advertising, no compromises. Your trust is our most valuable asset.

AI as Wisdom Amplifier

Artificial Intelligence should enhance human decision-making, not replace it. Ain, your AI companion, provides insights and suggestions while always respecting your autonomy. Every recommendation comes with explanations and sources.

Micro-Habit Revolution

Big changes come from small, consistent actions. We focus on 30-second interactions that compound into life-changing results. No feature ships without passing the "quick access" test.

Technical Architecture

Privacy-First, Local-First Architecture

Frontend

Flutter 3 for iOS, Android, macOS, and Web PWA

Local Storage

SQLite/Drift with SQLCipher encryption

State Management

Riverpod with encrypted Isar store

Backend

Supabase with Row Level Security

AI Processing

On-device TensorFlow-Lite + OpenAI/Gemini

Security

User-owned 256-bit encryption keys
